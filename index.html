With a desire to bring the best of the best in Hispanic foods to the local community, Rancho San Miguel Markets founders searched far and wide to find just the right combination of products and provide freshness beyond compare.  If you enjoy the Hispanic culture, you’ll simply be treated to an experience like no other at Rancho San Miguel Markets.  You’ll know right away that this isn’t your ordinary supermarket. 

Our produce department works hard to provide you with a wide variety of the most vibrant fruits and vegetables so that you can feel good about feeding your family-all within your budget.  Customer satisfaction is a high priority, that’s why we continually restock to make sure that you are getting the freshest produce. 

Stop by our full-service meat department to find the freshest quality meat and seafood.  You can shop with confidence knowing that our meat is always USDA inspected and is cut daily. Whether you’re looking for ready-to-eat, ready-to-make, or make-it-from-scratch, our full-service meat department has it all. 

Our bakery provides shoppers with traditional Hispanic pastries including concha, empanadas, fino, polvoron and much more.  We also can provide you with the perfect birthday, Quinceañera or wedding cake. 

If you are looking for an authentic Hispanic quick grab and go meal, the Kitchen will meet all of your breakfast, lunch and dinner needs. We offer breakfast burritos, family meals, combination plates and more.  We can even cater your next office lunch. 

Need to pick up some freshly made salsa for your next family gathering or celebration?  Stop by the Salsa Bar where you can choose from guacamole, rostisado, molcajete, salsa de arbol, salsa verde, ranchera or house made salsa.  Whatever kind you need-we have it.   In the mood for a refreshing Agua frescas?  Yep-we’ve got those too.  We offer horchata, watermelon, cucumber and lime, strawberry, pineapple, melon, tamarindo and Jamaica. Is seafood more your thing? Then you have to try our ceviches crab, fish campechana or camarones borrachos. Our Salsa Bar is like a store within the store! 

In addition to carrying the best variety of Hispanic items-we also offer your everyday items. Here at Rancho San Miguel Markets, we pride ourselves on shopping the competition for the best prices so you don’t have to. If you are in the neighborhood, give Rancho San Miguel Markets a try!
